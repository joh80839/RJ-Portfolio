My Top 5 movies (not in order)

  1. Interstellar

    It's an original story about space, time, and love.

  2. The Godfather

    An old school mafia movie that many consider a classic.

  3. Tenet

    A time traveling movie with good visuals and music

  4. Jason Bourne

    A thrilling movie about a spy who has lost his memory and goes through trials to try to get it back.

  5. Friday

    A comedy that tells the story of two guys trying to survive the day in their neighborhood.
